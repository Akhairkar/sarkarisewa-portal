About SarkariSewa Portal

SarkariSewa Portal is a free, independent guide that helps Indians find and understand government services — in plain Hindi and English, in one place.

Why this portal exists

Government services in India are spread across dozens of separate websites, each with its own layout, language and jargon. We built this portal to bring the essentials — eligibility, documents, fees, timelines and official links — for 92+ services into one consistent, bilingual format.

What we are (and are not)

We are an independent information guide, not a government body. We do not process applications, collect documents, or charge government fees. Every action link on this site takes you to the real official government website to complete your task there.

How our content is put together

Every service page is compiled from publicly available official government sources and cross-checked against the linked portal. Where a service is issued by state or municipal bodies instead of the centre, we say so and link to the relevant state directory.

Spotted a mistake?

Government rules, fees and portals change often, sometimes without notice. If something looks outdated or wrong, please tell us on the contact page — we'll get it corrected.
