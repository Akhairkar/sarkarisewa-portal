State-wise Services

Last updated: July 2026

Most central-government services (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, Passport, Driving Licence) use one national portal for every state. But some services are issued by state or municipal governments, so the exact application portal depends on where you live.

We link these services to national directory portals (e-District, CRS, NFSA) that route you to your state's system, since the exact state portal address differs across India. Select your state below, then open the service to see its national directory link and a note explaining the state-level variation.
