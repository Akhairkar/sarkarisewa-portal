Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

SarkariSewa Portal ("we", "us", "this portal") respects your privacy. This page explains what little data we collect, why, and your choices.

1. What we collect

This portal does not require you to create an account or submit personal documents to use it. We do not collect names, addresses, ID numbers or any government-document details — we only link out to official government websites where you may choose to enter such information directly with the government.

2. Local storage (on your device only)

We save your theme (light/dark) and language (Hindi/English) choice in your browser's local storage, so the site remembers your preference on your next visit. This data stays on your device and is never sent to us.

3. Cookies & analytics

If this site uses an analytics or advertising service (such as Google AdSense), that service may set its own cookies and collect data such as your IP address, browser type and pages visited, according to its own privacy policy. We do not control this data directly.

4. Third-party / government links

Every "Apply", "Download Form" and "Track Status" link on this portal takes you to an official government website that we do not operate. Once you leave SarkariSewa Portal, that website's own privacy policy applies — please read it before submitting any personal information.

5. Children's privacy

This portal is informational and not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Cookies

We use a small analytics cookie (Google Analytics 4) only to understand overall site usage — which pages are visited, how often, and roughly where from. This cookie is not set until you accept it via the consent banner shown on your first visit; if you reject or ignore it, no analytics cookie is set. You can change your choice at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this domain.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this page from time to time. Continued use of the portal after a change means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy, use the contact page linked in the footer.
